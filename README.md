# Idle Obelisk Miner Archaeology Optimizer

A small web tool for the Archaeology mode in Idle Obelisk Miner. You give it your
current stats and the reward you want more of, and it works out how to spend your
attribute points to get the most of it.

Open it here: https://caviarmy.github.io/archaeology-optimizer/

## How to use it

The page opens with a short setup. Pick the reward you are after, set your
ascension, then either type your stats in or upload a screenshot of your stats
screen and let the tool read them. It gives you a recommended attribute spread
and the numbers behind it. If you would rather set every field yourself, the full
view is one click away.

Everything runs in your browser, and your settings are remembered between visits.

## How it works

Rather than guess from damage alone, it simulates real runs for each possible
build and ranks them by what they actually earn. The question mark in the top
corner opens a page that lays out the formulas, the data behind them, and how the
results were checked.
